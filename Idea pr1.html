PROJECT 1: IDEA

COMPANIES

THERMAFROST

Thermafrost is a bottle company that creates metal bottles that are meant to keep your drinks cold for several hours. I plan on adding a color picking option that shows the bottle in many selectable colors.

BLUE BEAR

Blue Bear is a toy company that sells decorative plushies and other items to decorate your home with. A new toy is promoted every week on the front page.

PAWFUL BUDDIES

Pawful Buddies is a store for a variety of dog food and other dog necessities. This includes leashes, harnesses, dog toys, etc. The fourth page will be a reccomendation of fruits and vegetables dogs can eat safely.
